About SK8

Technoloy simplifies things, making life a more enjoyable, yet simpler one. Founded 26th August 2014, we made it our mission to innovate and quickly realize those technologies, and bring them into the lives of you and me.

We excel at writing softwares, more specifically building mobile apps and websites. Our projects includes iKardz, and Shelf. We also take development requests. Please contact us for more details.

We believe interest is the best teacher, and fun is the best motivator. Therefore we provide a fun working environment, and constantly look for, and team up people with potential and talents.
